应用简介 / About

“你今天想要做什么？” 是一款帮你找回专注的极简工具。它能有效减少手机成瘾和不必要的打扰，解决拖延问题，让你只聚焦于当下最重要的任务。

A minimalist tool designed to help you regain focus. It effectively reduces phone addiction and unnecessary distractions while tackling procrastination, keeping you focused on your most essential tasks.

核心理念 / Philosophy

我们相信，最好的效率工具不是塞满功能，而是帮你清空杂念。通过每日一问，引导你将精力投入到真正有意义的事情上。

We believe the best productivity tool shouldn't add features, but remove distractions. Through a simple daily prompt, we guide you to invest your energy into what truly matters.
